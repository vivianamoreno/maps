Four Must-See Museums In The Big Apple

The iconic city of New York City (a.k.a The Big Apple) twists and turns with endless spots to visit and admire. One of the best features of the city include the multitude of museums ranging from history to art. The map above highlights a few of the city's best art museums. The four museums are the Whitney Museum of American Art, the New Museum, the Museum of Modern Art (known popularly as the MET) and the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum.
